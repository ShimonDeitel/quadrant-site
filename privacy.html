Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Quadrant iPhone app ("Quadrant," "the app," "we," "us") handles information. The short version: Quadrant is built to collect essentially no personal data. It runs on your device, requires no account to use the core app, and contains no trackers, no advertising, and no third-party analytics or marketing SDKs.

Data we collect

Quadrant does not collect personal information about you for our own use. We do not run servers that gather your activity, we do not profile you, and we do not sell or rent data to anyone. Specifically:

  • No account is required to use the core matrix. You can add, organize, and complete tasks without signing up.
  • No advertising or tracking. There are no ad networks, no tracking pixels, no device-advertising identifiers, and no cross-app or cross-site tracking. Quadrant does not implement App Tracking Transparency tracking because it does not track you.
  • No third-party analytics or SDKs. We do not embed Google, Facebook, or other third-party analytics, attribution, or marketing kits.
  • No device permissions. Quadrant does not request access to your camera, photos, microphone, location, contacts, health data, motion data, files, or push notifications.

Data you enter stays on your device

The content you create in Quadrant — your task titles, which quadrant each task is in, completion state, dates, and recurring settings — is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's on-device data frameworks. Everything stays on your device. There is no account, no cloud sync, and no iCloud or CloudKit storage; the app does not transmit this content anywhere, and we cannot read it.

No account and no sync

Quadrant does not use accounts and does not offer Sign in with Apple. There is nothing to sign up for and nothing to log in to. The app does not sync your tasks across devices and does not store anything in iCloud or CloudKit — your matrix lives only on the iPhone where you created it.

Purchases

The optional Pro unlock is a one-time $0.99 in-app purchase processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles payment and entitlement; we never see your card number or billing details. We verify your Pro status through Apple's StoreKit on your device. Purchases are subject to Apple's terms and privacy practices.

How we use information

Because Quadrant does not collect your personal data, we do not use it for advertising, profiling, or resale. Any data the app handles exists solely to make the app work for you on your own devices.

Data sharing and disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade personal information with third parties. The only party involved in data handling is Apple (for App Store purchases) acting as an infrastructure provider under its own privacy terms. We may disclose information only if required to do so by a valid legal process, but in practice we hold essentially no personal data to disclose.

Data retention and deletion

Your task data is retained on your device until you delete it. You can delete any task inside the app, and you can remove all app data by deleting the app from your device. Because there is no account and no cloud storage, removing the app removes all of your Quadrant data.

Your privacy rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, and the right to data portability. Because your content stays entirely on your device, you can exercise these rights directly by viewing, editing, and deleting your data in the app. If you have any questions, contact us using the details below and we will respond consistent with applicable law.

Your privacy rights (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act, gives you rights to know what personal information is collected, to access and delete it, to correct it, and to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share your personal information, and we do not collect personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. You will never be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights. To make a request, contact us below.

Children's privacy

Quadrant is rated 4+ and is intended for a general audience. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 in violation of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) or similar laws. Because the app requires no account and collects essentially no personal data, it is appropriate for general use. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will address it promptly.
