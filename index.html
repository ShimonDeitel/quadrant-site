Download on the App Store

How it works

One idea: every task is either urgent, important, both, or neither. Quadrant makes that visible at a glance.

Sort into four boxes

Add a task and drop it into one of four quadrants by two axes — urgent or not, important or not. The most urgent and important work lands in a highlighted Do Now box.

Move as priorities shift

Priorities change through the day. Move any task between quadrants with a tap, complete it when it is done, and keep the matrix matching reality.

See the weekly trend

A weekly reflection shows how much landed in your win box — important work you got ahead of — versus the trap of urgent busywork that was never important.
